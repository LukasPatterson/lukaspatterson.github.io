About Lukas

Lukas Patterson blends synthwave, heavy rock, and shoegaze into a cinematic sound that hits as hard as it haunts. As the former lead guitarist of Star City Meltdown, he toured nationally throughout the 2010s alongside bands like Korn, Crossfade, Saliva, 10 Years, and Pop Evil—bringing lead guitar back to modern rock with full shred.

Now operating as a solo artist and producer, Lukas pushes the boundaries of alternative rock with immersive atmospheres, crushing 7- and 8-string guitar riffs, and vintage-inspired synths. Every release reflects a deep love for melody, drama, and unapologetic heaviness.
