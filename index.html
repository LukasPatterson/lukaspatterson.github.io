Lukas Patterson

Southwest Virginia native Lukas Patterson fuses crushing riffs, dreamy shoegaze textures, and retro 80s synth tones into a sound that hits as hard as it haunts. As a former touring guitarist and now solo artist, Lukas brings cinematic weight to modern rock. Always writing, always building, and always stoked to talk music.
